About Me.

I'm Lisette Mukiza, a Rwandan technologist and first-year student at ALCHE, driven by a simple belief: every child deserves the chance to turn their potential into power.

I build digital solutions that reach beyond Rwanda's cities into rural classrooms and public schools across Africa places where brilliant minds often go unnoticed. My work focuses on creating accessible technology that helps young people discover their talents, seize opportunities, and become the changemakers their communities need.

I believe the future of Africa isn't just in our capitals; it's in every village, every school, and every child who has been told to dream smaller. My goal is to prove them wrong, one solution at a time.

When I'm not coding or strategizing the next project, you'll find me talking to my plants (they're surprisingly good debugging partners) or whispering wisdom to dogs (they're better listeners than most humans)

Let's build a smarter, more connected Africa together.
