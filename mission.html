Empowering Africa's Future Through Education

OPINFINIA, I believe technology is the bridge that connects potential to opportunity. My mission is to empower African youth in rural public schools with tools that transform their talents into impact and their dreams into reality.

Mission

To empower African youth in rural public schools with technology that unlocks their potential, transforms their talents into tangible opportunities, and equips them to become changemakers who uplift their communities and shape Africa's future.

Aligned with the Global Challenge of Education

Across Africa, millions of young people still face barriers to quality education. OPINFINIA aligns with the Global Challenge of Education by providing innovative learning tools that promote access, inclusion, and lifelong growth.

My Impact Goal

  • Introduce digital learning devices in 20 rural schools within 3 years of project lauch.
  • Train 200 teachers in digital teaching methods.
  • Empower 5,000 students through different learning programs that will be design.

"Education is not a privilege - it's a bridge to freedom, and we're building it one school at a time."
